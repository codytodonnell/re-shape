IGS Re-Shape

Collaboratively and dynamically visualize your physical movement data over space and time.

Collect your movement data

Collect data on your physical movement across space and time. Visit the Re-Shape page to learn more about how to collect and process your movement data.

Visualize your data

Select one or more GPX, CSV, or JSON files with timestamped geographic coordinates of your movement to upload and visualize.

Check out the sample

This sample shows 8 pre-service teachers’ physical movement across Nashville, TN. The left view shows each teachers’ movement over a map while the right view extends their movement over over a timeline (approximately 1 week) where the vertical axis corresponds to the vertical dimension on the map.
